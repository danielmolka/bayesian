About

The financial services industry has undergone waves of changes in the past decade in response to a highly publicized financial meltdown, with the evaluation of a company’s risk management practices becoming an important factor in judging the stability of a financial institution. These recent changes have a left a gap between the type of candidates that an institution such as yours requires to satisfy these demands, and the type of individuals that aim to attain a placement at your institution. FARMSA recognizes this gap, and aims to host the Bayesian Case Competition in order to:

• Educate junior students on the importance of financial risk management
• Motivate students to consider internships and careers in risk management
• Connect students pursuing studies in related field with industry professionals
